Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

You can download JustTo from the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. Visit our home page and click on the appropriate download button for your device, or search for "JustTo" in your device's app store.

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and JustTo Wallet. All transactions are securely processed with 256-bit encryption.

Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking number via email and app notification. You can track your order directly in the app under "My Orders" or by clicking the tracking link in your shipping confirmation email.

We offer a 30-day hassle-free return policy. Items must be unused and in their original packaging. Simply initiate a return in the app under "My Orders" and follow the instructions. Return shipping is free for eligible items.
